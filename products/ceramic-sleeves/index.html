Product Description

Reliable Ceramic Solution for Precision Alignment

Ceramic sleeves are used to align ferrules and optical fibers inside adapters. Their dimensional accuracy and internal bore quality directly influence connection performance. With stable processing and strict quality control, our ceramic sleeves can meet the needs of communication-related applications requiring reliable precision ceramic components.
